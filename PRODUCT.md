# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and enterprise team leads looking for a Junior Business Analyst or Process Analyst who brings a unique blend of technical process mapping (BPMN, ADONIS) and design-conscious thinking.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Ondrej Michal Očkaj's skills, experience, and academic accomplishments (including his bachelor's thesis on enterprise digital technologies) through a highly polished, interactive portfolio. It demonstrates his ability to map processes (AS-IS/TO-BE) and design digital transformation solutions.

## Brand Personality
Analytical, Precise, Design-Conscious, Innovative. A refined blend of technical process systems and editorial aesthetics.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS templates (creamy/beige monoculture backgrounds, side-stripe borders, ghost cards with thin borders and huge shadows).
- Tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows on every section as default scaffolding.
- Amateurish sketchy SVG illustrations.

## Design Principles
- **Systematic Clarity**: Present process logic and metadata (BPMN diagrams, RACI matrices, Gantt charts) with crisp, industrial-grade accuracy.
- **Editorial Contrast**: Pair elegant display typography (Instrument Serif) with clean, readable sans-serif (Plus Jakarta Sans) to create a premium editorial feel.
- **Micro-Delight & Performance**: Use lightweight WebGL (Aurora, LiquidGlass) and fine interactive motion (from the `motion` package) that enhance the user experience without dragging down device performance.
- **Honest & Direct**: Focus on real deliverables, metrics, and case studies. Avoid marketing fluff or buzzword-heavy copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Keep text contrast ≥ 4.5:1 for body and ≥ 3:1 for large display elements.
- Implement `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` overrides for all WebGL and canvas-based animations.
- Native HTML structure and keyboard focus states are preserved and visible.
